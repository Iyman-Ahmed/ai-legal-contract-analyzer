EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of February 1, 2025, by and between:

InnovateTech Corp., a California corporation (the "Company"), and
Michael Chen (the "Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES

1.1 The Company hereby employs Employee as Senior Software Engineer, reporting to the VP of Engineering. Employee shall perform all duties assigned by the Company from time to time.

1.2 Employee shall devote their full business time, attention, and best efforts exclusively to the Company's business and shall not engage in any other employment or business activity without prior written approval.

2. COMPENSATION

2.1 Base Salary: $180,000 per year, payable bi-weekly in accordance with the Company's regular payroll practices.

2.2 Bonus: Employee shall be eligible for an annual performance bonus of up to 15% of base salary, subject to Company performance and individual performance criteria as determined by the Company in its sole discretion.

2.3 Equity: Employee shall receive a grant of 10,000 stock options vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff.

3. BENEFITS

3.1 Employee shall be entitled to participate in the Company's benefit plans as in effect from time to time, subject to plan terms and eligibility requirements.

4. AT-WILL EMPLOYMENT

4.1 Employment with the Company is at-will. Either party may terminate the employment relationship at any time, for any reason or no reason, with or without cause and with or without notice.

4.2 No severance shall be paid upon termination for any reason.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY

5.1 During and after employment, Employee shall maintain in strict confidence all Confidential Information of the Company and shall not disclose or use any Confidential Information except in the performance of Employee's duties.

5.2 "Confidential Information" includes all technical, business, financial, and other information of the Company, including source code, customer lists, business plans, financial data, and trade secrets, whether or not marked as confidential.

5.3 These confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of employment for a period of ten (10) years.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT

6.1 Employee hereby irrevocably assigns to the Company all inventions, discoveries, developments, improvements, trade secrets, and works of authorship conceived, made, developed, or reduced to practice by Employee, whether alone or jointly with others, during the term of this Agreement, whether or not during working hours, whether or not related to Company's business, and whether or not using Company resources.

6.2 This assignment includes all intellectual property rights worldwide. Employee waives all moral rights in such works.

6.3 Employee shall promptly disclose all inventions to the Company and execute all documents necessary to perfect the Company's ownership.

7. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

7.1 Non-Compete: During employment and for a period of two (2) years following termination for any reason, Employee shall not, directly or indirectly, engage in, own, manage, operate, control, be employed by, or provide services to any business that competes with the Company anywhere in the world.

7.2 Non-Solicitation of Employees: During employment and for two (2) years after termination, Employee shall not solicit, recruit, or hire any Company employee or encourage any Company employee to leave the Company.

7.3 Non-Solicitation of Customers: During employment and for two (2) years after termination, Employee shall not solicit, divert, or take away any Company customer or prospective customer.

8. REMEDIES

8.1 Employee acknowledges that a breach of this Agreement will cause irreparable harm to the Company and consents to entry of a temporary restraining order and injunction without posting bond.

8.2 In the event of any breach, Employee shall forfeit all unvested equity and repay any bonus received in the preceding twelve months.

9. GOVERNING LAW

9.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

9.2 Any disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration in San Francisco, California. EMPLOYEE WAIVES ALL RIGHTS TO A JURY TRIAL AND CLASS ACTION.

10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

10.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding the subject matter hereof.
